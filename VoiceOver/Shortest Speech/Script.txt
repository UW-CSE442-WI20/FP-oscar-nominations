This visualization presents data on the shortest Oscar acceptance speeches of all time as of 2019.

It shows pictures and some related information of the winners. The number indicates how many words are in the speech.

According to the source, the record for the shortest speech is held by Patty Duke, who simply said "thank you" when accepting her Best Supporting Actress award of 1962. 

Click on the image or the text, it will jump to the video of the speech.
